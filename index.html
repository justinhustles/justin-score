Justin Score (beta)




The Justin Score is a 0 to 10 rating that tells you how well a vehicle performs for the price you pay — based on either 0–60 mph or 1/4 mile time. 0 being the worst, 10 means you accidentally ordered a Dodge Demon instead of a ring again. (at least a car would never sleep with your homies).

Is that car you want fast as fuck for the price? Or is it a total ripoff? We all want a fast car for a good deal right? That’s exactly what this score answers.

The calculator multiplies your vehicle’s price by its time and compares that value to a benchmark. The higher the score, the better bang for your buck.

A score of 10 means unbeatable performance-per-dollar. A score below 5? You might be paying too much for too little.

Enjoy!
